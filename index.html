About IIT Jodhpur

Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur (IITJ), established in 2008, is one of the premier engineering and research institutions in India. Known for its innovative approach to research, IIT Jodhpur emphasizes interdisciplinary studies and state-of-the-art technologies. Situated in the vibrant city of Jodhpur, Rajasthan, IITJ has quickly gained recognition for its modern curriculum, collaborative research efforts, and cutting-edge facilities.
